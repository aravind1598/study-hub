Unit 4 — Combinatorics & Probability
Permutation, Combination, Binomial
▼
1
Permutation & Combination
nPr = n! / (n−r)!
nCr = n! / (r! × (n−r)!)
⭐ 5 marks
2
Binomial Theorem
(a+b)ⁿ = Σ nCr · aⁿ⁻ʳ · bʳ
General term: T(r+1) = nCr · aⁿ⁻ʳ · bʳ
⭐ 10 marks
∞
Unit 5 — Differential Calculus
Limits, Derivatives
▼
1
Limits
lim(x→0) sinx/x = 1
lim(x→0) (1+x)^(1/x) = e
lim(x→a) (xⁿ−aⁿ)/(x−a) = naⁿ⁻¹
⭐ 5 marks
2
Differentiation Rules
d/dx(xⁿ) = nxⁿ⁻¹
d/dx(sinx) = cosx | d/dx(cosx) = −sinx
Product: (uv)' = u'v + uv'
Quotient: (u/v)' = (u'v − uv')/v²
Chain: d/dx[f(g(x))] = f'(g(x))·g'(x)
⭐ Very Important
