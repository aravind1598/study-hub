▼

Hex digits: A=10,B=11,C=12,D=13,E=14,F=15

1's Complement: Flip all bits (0→1, 1→0)
2's Complement: 1's complement + 1

Binary Addition: 0+0=0, 0+1=1, 1+1=10(carry)

Q: Prove De Morgan's (A·B)'=A'+B'A=0,B=0: (0·0)'=1, 0'+0'=1 ✓

Ch 7 — Thermodynamics

Laws, Enthalpy, Entropy, Gibbs energy

▼
1st Law: ΔU = q + w | w = -PΔV
Enthalpy: ΔH = ΔU + ΔnRT
Hess's Law: ΔH = sum of ΔH of steps
Bond enthalpy: ΔH = Σ(bonds broken) - Σ(bonds formed)

Entropy: ΔS>0 → disorder increases
Gibbs energy: ΔG = ΔH - TΔS
ΔG<0 → spontaneous | ΔG>0 → non-spontaneous
ΔG=0 → equilibrium
